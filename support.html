‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a workout?
Go to the Workouts tab, tap the "+" button, and select "Start Empty Workout" or choose a program. Add exercises, log your sets, and tap "Complete Workout" when done.
How do I create a custom program?
Go to the Programs tab, tap the "+" button, and select "Create Custom Program". Add days, add exercises to each day, and save. You can then start workouts from your custom program.
Does my data sync across devices?
Yes! Your workout data automatically syncs to your private iCloud account. Sign in with the same Apple ID on all your devices to access your data everywhere.
Can I use the app offline?
Absolutely! All features work offline. Your workouts will sync to iCloud automatically when you're back online.
How do I watch exercise videos?
Tap on any exercise to view its details. If a video is available, you'll see a "Watch Video" button. Videos require an internet connection.
How do I change my weight units?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Fitness Profile ‚Üí tap to edit. You can switch between pounds (lbs) and kilograms (kg) at any time.
How do I track my body weight?
Go to the Progress tab and tap "Add Weight Entry". Your weight history will be displayed in a chart showing your progress over time.
Is my data private?
Yes! Your data is stored in your private iCloud account. We cannot access your workout data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
Are there any subscriptions or in-app purchases?
No! Lift Log is completely free with no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads. All features are available to everyone.
How do I delete my account?

To permanently delete your account and all data:

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  2. Scroll down to the "Account Actions" section
  3. Tap "Delete Account" (red button with trash icon)
  4. Confirm deletion twice (two-step confirmation to prevent accidents)

What gets deleted:

  • ‚úÖ All workout logs and exercise data
  • ‚úÖ All custom programs and routines
  • ‚úÖ All weight tracking entries
  • ‚úÖ Your user profile and preferences
  • ‚úÖ All data synced to iCloud

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to delete all your data before confirming.

Note: If you just want to sign out temporarily without deleting your data, use the "Sign Out" button instead.

üîß Troubleshooting

Data Not Syncing

If your data isn't syncing across devices:

  • Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices
  • Check that iCloud is enabled in Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud
  • Ensure you have an internet connection
  • Try force-closing and reopening the app

App Crashes or Freezes

If you experience crashes:

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS
  • Update to the latest version of Lift Log
  • Try restarting your device
  • Contact support if the issue persists

Exercise Videos Not Loading

If videos won't play:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  • Some exercises may not have videos available
